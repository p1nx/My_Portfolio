About Me



Hello World!
My name is Pinky. And for the umpteenth time, it is not my nickname! (:

I am a newbie to the world of front end web development. I ventured into this because I was frustrated with my job in an IT services company.


Other than training myself to be a full time web developer, I am an avid instagrammer. I love to click photographs of still life. Or make them pose to my liking. I also love to read and own a modest collection of novels to start a mini library of my own.
And yes, a doodler too.

When we are no longer able to change a situation - we are challenged to change ourselves.
- Viktor E. Frankl

It is tough to change into a new technology. There is so much to learn and my peers in this field are so much ahead of me. My current job is a stagnant place and I do not see myself going anywhere from here. This portfolio site is my attempt to set foot into becoming a full time front end developer. Like I said, I have only started the journey. This site has to go through a myriad of changes. But, one step at a time.


More about myself! I am a computer science graduate. For the past 5 years, I have been employed as an IBM Notes developer. And here I am, ready to turn my life around.
