About Me



My name is Pinky. (And it is not a nickname!)

I am a newbie to the world of front end web development. I ventured into this world frustrated with my job in an IT services company.
Other than convincing myself to train myself to be a full time web developer, I am an avid instagrammer.

I also love to read. I have the contents for a mini library but no cupboard as of yet.

And yes, a doodler too.
